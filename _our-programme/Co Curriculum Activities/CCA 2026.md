<h1><strong>Co-Curriculum Activities in Montfort Junior School</strong></h1>
<p>At Montfort, participation in a Co-Curricular Activity is strongly encouraged
for every student. Our core emphasis is on <strong>character development</strong> and
CCAs are vital for strengthening our school values.</p>
<p>Through their CCA, your son will have opportunities to:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>represent the school in competitions, performances and Values in Action
(VIA) projects, either as an individual or as part of a team.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>develop essential life skills such as <strong>resilience, confidence and community contribution</strong>.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>We are also pleased to share that <strong>Modular CCA</strong> has been
integrated into the curriculum for all Primary 3 to Primary 6 students,
held during curriculum time on Wednesday mornings.</p>
<p>Below are the CCAs available for different levels.</p>
