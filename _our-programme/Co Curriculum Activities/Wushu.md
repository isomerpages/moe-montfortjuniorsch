<h1><strong>WUSHU</strong></h1>
<p>Wushu – the traditional Chinese martial art that blends physical skill
with artistic expression is one of the school’s new co-curricular activities.
More than just a sport, Wushu CCA helps students to develop holistic fitness,
strong values and a deeper appreciation for Chinese culture and heritage.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Members of our CCA can expect exciting training sessions that instil perseverance,
respect for self and others and self-discipline through consistent practice.
Members will learn to foster teamwork and learn sportsmanship during group
training sessions.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Beyond training, our CCA also places emphasis on fostering the holistic
development of our members.</p>

<h3>EVENTS</h3>
<p><strong>Prize Giving Day:</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>Showcase of Excellence</strong>
</p>
<p>Prize Giving Day is when our Wushu students dazzle the entire school community
with their martial arts mastery. This is their moment to demonstrate the
grace, power, and precision they've developed through dedicated training.</p>
<p><strong>Character in Motion</strong>
</p>
<p>Guided by CHRISTE values, our performers give their absolute best with
discipline and respect. Every movement showcases not just physical skill,
but the character and commitment that defines a true Montfortian.</p>
